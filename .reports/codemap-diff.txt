## Architecture Changes

1. **Admin Permission System**: Full RBAC implementation with fine-grained permissions for nodes, VMs, plans, templates, IP sets, customers, backups, and settings.

2. **Console Token System**: Time-limited token-based access for VNC and serial consoles, allowing secure delegation.

3. **ISO Management**: Complete ISO lifecycle management for customers (upload, attach, detach, delete).

4. **Failover Request Tracking**: New endpoints for tracking and managing failover operations.
